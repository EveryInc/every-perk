## Customer Advisory Board — Notes from March 14 Session

**Attendees:** GlobalTech Inc, Meridian Systems, BrightPath Consulting, Apex Dynamics, NordTech GmbH

**Key themes:**

1. **Automation is the #1 request.** Every customer independently mentioned wanting less manual work in expense submission. "If you can auto-categorize and auto-match receipts, that alone justifies our subscription."

2. **Policy transparency matters.** Multiple customers said their employees don't understand why expenses get flagged. They want clearer violation messages with the specific policy cited.

3. **Enterprise scale concerns.** GlobalTech (2,400 travelers) and Meridian (1,800) both raised performance issues with large reports and large group bookings.

4. **Competitive pressure from Navan.** Three of five customers mentioned evaluating Navan as an alternative. Key differentiator they cited: "Navan's mobile experience feels faster." One customer said their contract renewal depends on Perk shipping AI categorization before Q3.

5. **Integration wishlist.** Top requests: direct SAP integration (3 of 5), Slack notifications for approvals (4 of 5), calendar sync for trip planning (2 of 5).
