### Section 2: How It Works

**Headline:**
Three levels of AI confidence. Zero guesswork.

**Step 1: Auto-Applied (85%+ confidence)**
The AI is confident. The category appears pre-filled with a subtle AI badge. Your team just taps "Submit." No thinking required.

**Step 2: Suggested (60-85% confidence)**
The AI has a few good ideas. Three category suggestions appear as tappable pills. Pick the right one in a single tap. Takes 2 seconds instead of 30.

**Step 3: Manual Fallback (Under 60% confidence)**
The AI isn't sure? No problem. The standard category picker appears, same as today. No degradation — just business as usual for the rare edge case.

**Design note:** Include animation showing all three states in sequence.

### Section 3: Features

**Headline:**
Built for the way your team actually works.

**Feature tiles:**

1. **Receipt Intelligence**
Snap a photo. Our OCR extracts merchant, amount, currency, and date. The AI uses this context to predict the right category. Works in 42 currencies.

2. **Learning System**
Every time someone overrides a suggestion, the AI learns. Your auto-categorization gets more accurate over time — tailored to YOUR company's spending patterns.

3. **Finance-Team Friendly**
Every AI-assigned category is flagged with an audit trail. Your finance team knows exactly which categories were auto-applied, suggested, or manually entered. Full transparency.

4. **Policy-Aware**
Smart Expenses knows your company's spend policies. If an expense needs approval, the AI tells the submitter upfront — no surprises at review time.
